Random graph visualizer

This applet provides a visual representation of the Erdos-Renyi G(n,p) random graph model by finding a MST of the graph (with edges weighted by probability) which it then feeds into a force-directed graph layout algorithm, giving a relatively clean and uncluttered view for low values of p.

Lenore Cowen and Greg Aloupis at Tufts provided suggestions, inspiration, and feedback, and I worked with Michael Gold on a related project for a class.
